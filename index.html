How does it work?

Step 1: Inquire  - #top

Let me know you are interested in my service with the following information: 

• What type of repair or service is needed to be performed. 

• Your vehicle's make/Year/Model/mileage
Optional:
Include any other helpful information such as any symptoms (noise, vibration, leak, or etc). Providing photos or videos would also help in great deal




Step 2: Estimate - #top

During the estimatimation, feel free to ask any question you may have about the estimate. 

An estimate with a few options will be sent for you to choose from. It includes:  

• Parts options recommendation - You choose your own part by brand or price. 

• Labor hour estimate - This is fee that I'll charge for the service.



Step 3: Commit and buy parts - #top

If everything looks good on estimate, then proceed. 

• I'll send you informatino on where and how to order parts onilne for the service. 

• At this step, I'll also provide my affiliation promotion code for additional discount.  

• Purchase the parts. And have the parts delivered to your home



Step 4: Schedule - #top

Make an appointment with me. 

Once parts've arrived, please contact me with your appointment date/time of your preference:  

• Provide two dates/times (e.g., one preffered and another as secondary).

• Once the appt is confirmed, I'll send you my location information.



Step 5: Bring in for Service - #top

Bring your car to my garage on the appointment day. 

• Arrive on time. Let me know you've arrived (if you're running late).

• You can either drop off and return at the time of completion or you can wait onsite. 
  There is a beautiful waterfall by the Los Penasquitos trail close by. 
  It's a really nice trail for walk or bike. :) 

• Once the job is complete, you should receive a notification from me. 

• Payment is due at the delivery (Venmo, Zelle, or cash). 

• Enjoy the car!
