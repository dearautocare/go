Quality Repairs at Affordable Prices!

Welcome to my website. I'm Zach, and I'm offering reliable repairs and maintenance such as oil changes, brake repairs, diagnostics, (and more!) right from my garage.

• Do you need car repairs but don't have time for the shop?
• Do you want to get your car running smoothly without the hassle?
• Do you want to skip the expensive shops!

Then contact me and get personalized service with honest work at a fraction of the cost at your convenience.

Note: I offer mechanic labor service only. I don’t typically provide part for the service. So make sure to purchase your parts needed and bring them with you on your day of appointment. I'm happy to recommend what parts to purchase for your needs through my affiliated parts network for free. So let me know if that's what you're instersted in. Or if you already have parts in-hand; then, please reach out and tell me before making an appointment so I can verify the fitment.

How does it work?

Step 1: Inquire  - #top

Let me know you are interested in my service with the following information:

• What type of repair or service is needed to be performed.

• Your vehicle's Make/Year/Model/Mileage (or VIN if available). 

• Optional: Include any other helpful information such as any symptoms (noise, vibration, leak, or etc). Providing any photo or video would also help in great deal.




Step 2: Estimate - #top

During the estimatimation, feel free to ask any question you may have .

An estimate with a few options will be sent for you to choose from. It includes:

• Parts options recommendation - You choose your own part by brand or price.

• Labor hour estimate - This is the fee that I'll charge for the service.




Step 3: Commit and buy parts - #top

If everything looks good on estimate, then proceed.

• I'll send you the information on where and how to order parts onilne for the service.

• At this step, I'll also provide my affiliated promo code for additional discount.

• Purchase the parts. And have them delivered to your home.




Step 4: Schedule - #top

Once parts've arrived, please contact me with your appointment date/time of your preference:

• Provide two date/time set (e.g., a preferred date/time and another as secondary).

• Once the appointment is confirmed, I'll send you my location information.




Step 5: Bring-in for Service - #top

Bring your car to my garage on the appointment day.

• Arrive on time. Let me know you've arrived (if you're running late).

• You can either drop-off and return at the time of completion. Or you can wait onsite.
There is a beautiful waterfall by the Los Penasquitos trail close by. It's a really nice trail for a walk or bike. :)

• Once the job is complete, you should receive a notification from me.

• Payment is due at the delivery (Venmo, Zelle, or cash).

• Enjoy the car!
